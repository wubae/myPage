Wuuli Strength


Wuuli Strength is an online powerlifting, strength & conditioning service catering to individuals seeking to enhance their performance in the gym and on the platform. Wuuli Strength's coaching philosophy centers on creating personalized training programs tailored to each individual's unique needs and goals. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced lifter, we will work with you to develop a program that will help you build strength, improve your technique, and achieve your desired results.

About Me

Growing up, I always had a difficult time finding a sport that was appealing and accessible to me. It wasn't until early in high school I became obsessed with progress and results in the gym. The aspect of training and results being enumerated has fascinated me. I eventually discovered powerlifting and created the first powerlifting club in our high school with a group of friends. I did my first powerlifting meet in 2017 and fell in love with the sport. It wasn't until in college I started to offer coaching for free to my fellow peers getting into the sport. I found coaching to be very rewarding and only wanted to further my craft. I indulged myself with online resources, hands-on experience in powerlifting meets, and always trying to learn from others.
